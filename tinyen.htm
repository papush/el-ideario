General Summary

At the moment Pablo is one step away from finishing the construction of his tiny house. The only thing left to do is to build a trailer with the exact dimensions of the house in order to mount it and so it becomes a mobile tiny house!

With this crowdfunding campaign we intend to collect the modest sum of $400 in order to kickstart the last part of this process. It's not just about Papus's personal motives, this whole endeavor is about generating social change, promoting more sustainable and affordable housing options in Costa Rica by setting the example, and further on setting up a tiny house building cooperative.

At the same time the objective is intimately related to the EcoVillage the we're building, from this collective effort we aim to generate a series of projects that will empower the local communities of Cartago in several ways, such as: creating a solidarity economy, community organic gardens, networks for mutual aid and the opening of spaces for interactive education.

How will the funds be used?

The funds collected with this campaign will be used in the following way:

  • Pay the last part of the Trailer - $200

  • Towing the house (car rental) - $150

  • safety fund for unexpected expenses - $50
